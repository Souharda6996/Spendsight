## Interview Guide

**Message to send:**
> "Hey, building a free tool that audits AI subscriptions (Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT etc.) for small teams. Would love 10 mins of your time this week — just chatting about how your team uses these tools. No sales pitch."

**Questions:**
1. What AI tools does your team pay for right now?
2. Do you know off the top of your head your monthly AI spend?
3. Have you ever compared plans or tried to optimize this?
4. What would make you actually use (or ignore) an audit tool like this?
5. What's the most surprising way your team actually uses AI tools day-to-day?
