## FAQ

**Q: Is this free?**  
A: Yes, completely free. No credit card, no account, no catch. SpendSight is a free tool built by Credex to help startups find waste in their AI subscriptions.

**Q: What data do you store?**  
A: We store your tool selections, plan choices, and spend amounts to generate your shareable report. We never store personal information unless you choose to enter your email. You can share your audit link with anyone — it contains no personally identifiable information.

**Q: How accurate are the recommendations?**  
A: Very accurate for the rules they cover. Every recommendation is based on published pricing from the tools' official pages (verified against live URLs). We don't invent savings — if your stack is already optimized, we'll tell you that clearly. The AI summary is a readable narrative, not the source of the math.

**Q: What is Credex?**  
A: Credex is a startup that sources discounted AI credits from companies that over-forecasted their usage. When a company buys $500k in Anthropic credits and only uses $300k, Credex finds buyers for the remainder. SpendSight helps identify teams who could benefit from this kind of optimization. We'll only reach out if your savings are significant and you choose to enter your email.

**Q: How do shareable links work?**  
A: Every audit gets a unique 10-character ID (e.g., `/audit/abc1234def`). This link is publicly accessible — anyone with the link can view the results. No login required. The link contains no personal information; it only shows the tools, plans, and savings numbers you entered. Your email (if provided) is never visible in the shared link.
